Research

At MDS group, we focus on Artificial Intelligence and Perception for the Marine Sciences and Environments, in particular at the interface of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, Robotics and Machine Learning. We therefore need to deal with challenging but very interesting real-world images acquired by deep-sea robots.
